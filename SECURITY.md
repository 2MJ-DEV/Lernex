## Report a Vulnerability

> **Note:** We do not have a bug bounty program.

The **Lernex** team takes all security vulnerabilities very seriously. Thank you for contributing to the improved security of our open-source project. We appreciate your transparency and efforts, and we are committed to **recognizing your contribution**. If you discover a security vulnerability, please **contact us directly by email** at: **julesmukadi.dev@gmail.com** We will respond as soon as possible and work with you to resolve the issue.
