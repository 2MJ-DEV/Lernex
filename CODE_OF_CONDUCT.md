## CODE OF CONDUCT 

The **Lernex** online communities aim to be **friendly, inclusive, and constructive** spaces where everyone can learn Next.js, ask questions, or simply exchange ideas. To ensure a positive experience for all members, we ask everyone to **respect the following rules**.

## Be Polite, Welcoming, and Inclusive
- Any inappropriate behavior or harassment, in any form, is **strictly prohibited**.
- Comments or actions designed solely to **provoke or troll** will not be tolerated and will be immediately stopped.
- Never post **personal information** about a member or links to such information.

## Our Standards
Here are some examples of behaviors that foster a positive environment:

- Use **inclusive and welcoming language**.
- Respect **different viewpoints and experiences**.
- Graciously accept **constructive criticism**.
- Focus on **the best interests of the community**. - Show **empathy** towards other members.

## Our Responsibilities
The **maintainers and administrators** of Lernex are responsible for clarifying the standards of behavior and taking **appropriate and fair action** in the event of a breach of the code of conduct.

They have the right to:
- Delete, modify, or reject any non-compliant comment, contribution, code, issue, or pull request.
- Temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for inappropriate, threatening, or harmful behavior.

## Reporting

**julesmukadi.dev@gmail.com** After reviewing each report, the team will respond in the manner it deems appropriate. The team guarantees the **confidentiality** of the person who reported the incident.
